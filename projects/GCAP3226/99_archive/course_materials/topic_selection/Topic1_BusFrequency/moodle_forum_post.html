🚌 Bus Frequency Optimization Project

GCAP 3226: Empowering Citizens through Data

Topic 1 • Group Work (4-6 students) • Advanced Data Governance Analysis

🎯 Project Overview

Analyze how Hong Kong's Transport Department uses data to make bus frequency and route modification decisions, and develop optimization recommendations.

This project offers an exciting opportunity to evaluate current data governance practices in public transport planning. You'll examine how ridership data, peak/off-peak patterns, and route performance metrics influence both frequency decisions and route modifications, including analysis of the 2024-25 North District bus route consultation proposals.

🚀 Head Start: What We've Already Done

✅ Data Request Submitted: Official information request has been sent to the Transport Department
✅ Consultation Document Available: 2024-25 North District bus route planning consultation paper with proposed frequency and route changes
✅ Research Framework: Initial research questions and methodology established for analyzing route optimization decisions

🎉 Your Team's Advantage:

Skip the initial bureaucratic hurdles! You can dive straight into data analysis and policy evaluation while waiting for additional Transport Department responses.

🔍 Data Governance Analysis Opportunities

📊 Evaluate Current Practices

🎯 Decision-Making Transparency: Assess how the Transport Department uses ridership data to justify frequency changes
🎯 Data Quality Assessment: Evaluate the completeness and accuracy of passenger counting systems
🎯 Public Consultation Integration: Analyze how passenger feedback is incorporated into frequency decisions
🎯 Performance Metrics: Identify gaps between stated optimization goals and actual implementation

📈 Key Research Questions

❓ Optimization Effectiveness: To what extent might current frequency decisions align with actual ridership patterns?
❓ Data-Driven Gaps: What additional data sources could potentially improve frequency optimization decisions?
❓ Route Planning Analysis: How might route modifications and frequency adjustments in the 2024-25 consultation paper affect service efficiency?
❓ Policy Recommendations: What improvements could potentially enhance the Transport Department's data governance for route and frequency planning?
