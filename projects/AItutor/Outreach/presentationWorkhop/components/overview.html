Workshop Overview

About this Workshop: The flipped classroom approach has been actively promoted at Hong Kong Baptist University (HKBU), particularly among General Education (GE) course instructors seeking to enhance student engagement and learning outcomes.

1 Understand the flipped classroom model and its benefits for student engagement
2 Explore AI-powered tools for creating engaging video content
3 Learn about streaming avatars and their applications in education
4 Develop practical implementation strategies for your courses

Workshop Structure

This workshop will guide you through the process of implementing flipped classroom methodology using cutting-edge AI tools. We'll cover everything from content creation to student engagement strategies.
