📅 Phased Implementation Approach

Success in AI integration comes from thoughtful, gradual implementation. Here's your step-by-step roadmap:

1

Week 1-2: Foundation & Pilot

🎬 Create your first AI-generated video for one lecture
👥 Set up HeyGen account and avatar selection
📊 Establish baseline metrics (current preparation time)
🎯 Deploy to small student group for feedback
2

Month 1: Expansion & Optimization

🤖 Implement streaming avatar for Q&A support
📈 Analyze student engagement and learning outcomes
💻 Try vibe coding for custom educational widgets
🔄 Refine workflows based on initial results
3

Semester 1: Full Integration

🎓 Scale to entire course portfolio
📚 Integrate with LMS and existing workflows
👨‍🏫 Share best practices with colleagues
🏆 Document success stories and ROI
