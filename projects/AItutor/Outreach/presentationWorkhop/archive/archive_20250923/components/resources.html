üîß Troubleshooting & FAQ

Common issues and solutions:

‚ùì Video generation is taking longer than expected
HeyGen processing times vary based on video length and complexity. Typical processing is 3-5 minutes for standard educational content. Check your account status and try reducing video complexity if needed.
‚ùì Avatar responses seem inaccurate or off-topic
Ensure your knowledge base is properly configured with course materials. The avatar's accuracy depends on the quality and relevance of uploaded content. Consider adding more specific course documents and FAQs.
‚ùì Students can't access the streaming avatar
Verify LMS integration settings and check student permissions. Ensure the avatar link is properly embedded and students have the necessary access rights in your course.
‚ùì Vibe coding session isn't working as expected
Make sure you're using clear, specific language when describing your requirements. Break complex requests into smaller, manageable steps. Check your AI assistant's context and provide examples when possible.
