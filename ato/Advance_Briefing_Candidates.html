Test Overview

Duration: 2 hours (test) + 10 minutes (presentation) + Q&A

Assignment Focus: Review and Remix

We want to set clear expectations for this test. Your primary goal is to demonstrate a deep understanding of the existing code and how to optimize it.

  • Review: Analyze the architecture, code quality, and structure of the provided codebase.
  • Remix: You are expected to "remix" the code. This means focusing on improving the existing architecture or optimizing performance/maintainability, rather than building extensive new features (like new pages) from scratch. A small functional change may be required to demonstrate your understanding, but the priority is on architectural insight.

What to Expect

During the test, you will:

  1. Review the codebase using any tools you prefer.
  2. Analyze the overall structure and technical framework.
  3. Implement an optimization or minor enhancement to demonstrate your "remix".
  4. Prepare a 10-minute presentation covering:
  5. Your analysis of the current structure.
  6. Your proposed optimizations or architectural improvements.
  7. A demo of your "remix" (the changes you made).
  8. How you would explain these technical concepts to non-technical staff.

Hint: You are expected to use vibe coding tools such as AI agents in your IDE.
