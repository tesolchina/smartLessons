1. Elements of an Effective Policy Ask

A strong policy ask should be clear, specific, and actionable. Let's identify the key components:

Key Elements:

  • Who: Identify the specific decision-maker or body
  • What: State exactly what action you want them to take
  • Why: Provide compelling reasons and evidence
  • When: Include a timeframe or deadline if relevant
