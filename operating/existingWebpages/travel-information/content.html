Transportation Guide

The following suggestions provide different ways¹ to reach Padma & Hari Harilela Lecture Theatres (夏利萊博士演講廳), which is inside the Lam Woo International Conference Centre (林護國際會議中心), Shaw Campus, Hong Kong Baptist University (HKBU), Hong Kong: 
Routes to the Conference Venue (from Shaw Campus)

Note¹: Download the Conference Venue Map to show it to the taxi driver when taking a taxi to the conference location.

From Kowloon Tong MTR Station to Hong Kong Baptist University

Walking (~ 800 meters)
Green MiniBus (Route 25MS)
Urban Taxi (Red Taxi)
Walking (~ 800 meters)
Green MiniBus (Route 25MS)

25MS: Circular Line between Kowloon Tong MTR Station and HKBU

  • Fare: HK$5.1
  • Boarding stop: Public Transport Interchange (MTR Exit D or B2)
  • Drop-off stop: Baptist University Road Campus, Baptist University Road (4th station)
  • After getting off, cross the road and walk along Renfrew Road for approximately 1 minute to reach the workshop venue
  • Note: You may take Route 25M (Direction: Tung Tau Estate) alternatively. (The boarding and drop-off stops are the same, and the fare is HK$ 6.3)
Urban Taxi (Red Taxi)
  • Fare: ~ HK$ 27
  • A taxi stand is available on To Fuk Road, next to Kowloon Tong MTR Exit F

From Hong Kong International Airport to Hong Kong Baptist University

Bus E22 (~45 minutes)
Urban Taxi (Red Taxi)
Bus E22 (~45 minutes)
E22: operated by CityBus
  • Fare: HK$18.6
  • Direction: Lam Tin (North)
  • Boarding stop: Airport (Ground Transportation Centre)
  • Drop-off stop: Kam Shing Road, Junction Road (Station 17)
  • After getting off, cross Junction Road and walk along Renfrew Road until you reach the Conference venue.
Urban Taxi (Red Taxi)
  • Duration: ~30 minutes
  • Fare: ~K$240-290

Other options

Please see below some other recommended hotels (~ a 15-Minute Taxi Ride¹) which you can book directly.

Alva Hotel by Royal - Shatin

MTR:
Bus:

MTR:

Take the Tuen Ma Line from City One Station to Tai Wa Station (3 stops), then take the East Rail Line to Kowloon Tong Station, or interchange at Diamond Hill Station and get off at Lok Fu Station. Walk for about 14 minutes from either of the stations or take a short taxi ride to reach HKBU.
