Video: Cantonese Courses and Enhancement Services

Description: As an international student studying in Hong Kong, the courses and Language Enhancement Services offered by the Language Centre have allowed me to improve my Cantonese language skills. I would strongly recommend international students to enrol in these courses and Language Enhancement Services to enrich their studies in HKBU and their time in Hong Kong.
