English Across the Curriculum (EAC) - Workshops and Services on Offer

EAC provides English for specific purposes (ESP) language enhancement workshops and services to all departments at HKBU. We customise workshops and services for students and teachers. EAC includes the design, development and delivery of tailor-made:

 

  • lectures/workshops/seminars, and

  • consultations for individuals and small groups.

 

Please contact the Language Centre if you are interested in commissioning our EAC workshops and services to cater for the specific language needs of your students.

 

Comments from participating Departments:

“The EAC Workshop has supported students’ academic writing skills. Assignments are of better-structured essays than in previous years.” (Academy of Visual Arts)

“The EAC initiative which had set out to address the specific disciplinary English language learning needs of students’ generally met my course expectations.” (Department of Education Studies)

“I found the workshop was useful for students. Some of them did apply what they learnt from the workshop to their written assignments.” (Department of Religion and Philosophy)
