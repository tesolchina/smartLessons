DIGITAL CREDENTIAL PROGRAMME


About the Programme

The Language Centre of Hong Kong Baptist University (HKBU) has partnered with Credly as the first higher education institution in Hong Kong to introduce the digital credential scheme. The Credly platform and its digital badging tools are used as a framework of recognizing and validating your skills and allow you to create a verified form of digital learning portfolio as part of a competency-based, student-centred approach to language learning. You are encouraged to utilise the platform to document your language learning journey and celebrate your achievements throughout your years of studies at HKBU.

List of Badges and Eligibility

Overview: Language Centre issues digital badges for three types of credentials to eligible students. There are different levels of engagement expected for each type of credentials in the programme, and students will have to fulfil the respective requirements in order to claim a digital badge.

Language Centre of Hong Kong Baptist University X Credly

Diamond badges: Professional contribution to LC events

Students who have demonstrated exceptional professionalism and made significant contributions to Language Centre events are eligible to receive the prestigious Diamond badges. These badges are awarded to individuals who have gone above and beyond in their involvement and have made a noteworthy impact on the success of LC events.

Golden badges: Contribution to LC services

Students who participated in or contributed to competitions or other services organised by the Language Centre are eligible to receive the badges for their commitment to fostering the language-learning community.

Silver badges: Voluntary Supplementary Learning

Students who completed supplementary courses, tutorial programmes and workshops and met certain criteria are eligible to receive the badges for their self-initiated efforts for language learning (The criteria vary across different courses, workshops and programmes).

Bronze badges: Required or Elective Courses

Students who earn an A- or above in the required or elective credit-bearing courses are eligible to receive the badges for their excellent performance.


How to apply
    • Step 1: Students who are eligible for the badges will receive an email invitation to fill out a form where they can confirm their intention to take the badge.
    • Step 2: Successful applicants will receive an email notification from Credly to create an account and claim the badge. For details on accepting a badge, please refer to the instructions provided by Credly here.
    • Step 3: Badge recipients are encouraged to share their digital badges on social media platforms, such as LinkedIn, Instagram, and Facebook with hashtags #HKBULanguageCentre #HKBULCdigitalcredential #HKBULCbadges to celebrate their achievements, expand their networks, and connect with peers!
