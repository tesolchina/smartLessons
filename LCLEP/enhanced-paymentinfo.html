Payment of Course Fees

Step 1: Make Payment

After registering for a course online, please pay the course fee using one of the following methods:

  • ATM transfer
  • E-banking
  • Bank counter

Note: Payment via PPS or Credit Card is NOT accepted.

Step 2: Bank Account Details

üè¶ Hang Seng Bank Account

Account Number: 283 - 338366 - 015
Account Name: Hong Kong Baptist University

Important: If you pay through e-banking, please select "Transfer" rather than "Bill Payment" and include your student No. and Course Code under the "Reference to Payee" section.

Step 3: Submit Payment Proof

Please write down the following information on the receipt/payment proof and submit it to the General Office of the Language Centre within two working days after receiving the email notification about course fee payment:

  • Full Name in English
  • Student No. / Staff No.
  • Course Code
  • Section No.

Important: If you have registered for more than one course, please provide a receipt for each course. If the receipt/payment proof is not received by the Language Centre by the respective deadline, your place will be allocated to the next student/staff on the waiting list without prior notice.

Step 4: COVID-19 Temporary Arrangements

Please email the receipt/payment proof to lcsup@hkbu.edu.hk within 2 working days.

Email Subject Format: COURSE FEE PAYMENT: [course code], [section code], [student ID]

Step 5: Check Payment Status

After submission of your payment receipt, you may check your payment status and enrollment status of the course after three working days by clicking on "My Courses" on the registration system.

Course Fee Refund Applications

UGC-funded students who have attended at least 85% of lessons of Chinese, English or Putonghua Supplementary Courses are eligible to apply for a full refund of course fees before a specified deadline.

View Refund Details ‚Üí

Note: The course fee of supplementary foreign language course is non-refundable.

Certificate Applications

Students who have fulfilled the attendance requirement of supplementary courses may apply for a Certificate of Attendance. The completion of these courses will NOT be listed on undergraduate students' academic transcripts.

Students who have passed PUTO1300/1400/2270/2300/2400/3080/3280 may apply for a Certificate of Course Completion. The completion of these courses will continue to be listed on undergraduate students' academic transcripts.
