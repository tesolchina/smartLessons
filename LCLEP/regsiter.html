✅ Course Eligibility

🎓 UGC-Funded Students

All courses are open to UGC-funded undergraduate and postgraduate degree students as stipulated by the UGC.

Eligible for All Courses

🌍 Foreign Language Courses

Foreign language courses (French, German, Japanese, Korean and Spanish) can be taken by non UGC-funded undergraduate and postgraduate degree students.

Limited Eligibility

🚫 Exclusions

Students from the School of Continuing Education are excluded from foreign language course enrollment.
