üìù To Enroll in/Register for a Course/Workshop

Step 1: Login

Log in to the system through your SSOID

Login URL: https://lclep.hkbu.edu.hk/sup

Method: Use your HKBU Single Sign-On credentials

Step 2: Select Course

Click "Enroll Courses" and select your desired course/workshop

What happens next:

  • You will receive an email about the acknowledgement of your registration
  • If the class is full and you have enrolled in the waiting list, an email will be sent to you about your enrollment on the waiting list
  • Another email will be sent to you if a place is later allocated to you

Step 3: Pay Course Fee

If you are eligible to take the course/workshop, you will receive an email about payment instructions

Payment Timeline: Please pay the course fee and submit the receipt within 2 working days

Payment Instructions: View Payment Information ‚Üí

Note: This step is not required for free-of-charge courses/workshops

Step 4: Enrollment Confirmed

After submission of the receipt, or if it is a free-of-charge course/workshop, you will receive an email about your successful enrollment

Confirmation Timeline: Within 2 working days

Next Steps: Please attend the course/workshop accordingly

Step 5: ZOOM Classes

If the courses/workshops are conducted via ZOOM, you will receive a ZOOM hyperlink from the course instructor

Timeline: Before classes start

Source: Direct from course instructor

Step 6: Course Cancellation Notice

If a course/workshop is cancelled due to insufficient enrollment, you will receive an email notification

Notice Period: Three working days prior to the start of the course/workshop at the latest

‚ùå To Drop a Course

Step 1: Contact Support

Currently, students are not able to drop a course from the system directly

Action Required: Please email lcsup@hkbu.edu.hk

Email Subject Format: Drop a course; [Course Code], [Section Code], [Student ID]

Step 2: Review Refund Policy

If you would like to drop a course but the course fee has already been paid, please read the guidelines on course fee refunds

Refund Information: View Refund Guidelines ‚Üí
