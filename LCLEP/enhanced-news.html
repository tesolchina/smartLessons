Course Eligibility Information

Please note the following important information regarding course enrollment eligibility:

🎓 UGC-Funded Students

All courses are open to UGC-funded undergraduate and postgraduate degree students as stipulated by the UGC.

Eligible
🌍 Foreign Language Courses

Foreign language courses (French, German, Japanese, Korean and Spanish) can be taken by non UGC-funded undergraduate and postgraduate degree students.

Limited Eligibility
🚫 Exclusions

Students from the School of Continuing Education are excluded from foreign language course enrollment.

Not Eligible

📋 Enrollment Guidelines

✅ Who Can Enroll

  • UGC-funded undergraduate students
  • UGC-funded postgraduate students
  • Non UGC-funded students (foreign language courses only)

❌ Who Cannot Enroll

  • School of Continuing Education students (for foreign language courses)
  • Non-degree students (for most courses)
