Course Overview

Course Title: GCAP3226 - Empowering Citizens Through Data: Participatory Policy Analysis for Hong Kong
Instructors: Dr. Talia Wu (Math Department, taliawu17@hkbu.edu.hk), Dr. Simon Wang (Language Centre, simonwang@hkbu.edu.hk)
Course Objectives: This course equips students with analytical tools and interdisciplinary approaches for data-informed policymaking in Hong Kong. It integrates quantitative and qualitative insights to address social issues aligned with UN SDGs, evaluates government data use for transparency, and engages students in practical projects for experiential learning.
